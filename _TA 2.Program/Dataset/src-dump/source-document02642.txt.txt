aVThe dance department at Connecticut College has established a philosophical framework supported by its curriculum and activities in response to the present state of the education of the arts including multiculturalism and internationalization. The approach to education is based on "ways of knowing, thinking, doing and creating."\u000a

aVThe centerpiece of the department is the recognition of the importance of an individual's interaction with the world as a whole person, not just as an artist. The late artist and educator Joseph Beuys once said: "A total work of art is only possible in the context of the whole of society."\u000a
